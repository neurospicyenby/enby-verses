Threads Unravelled

Published on 28th October 2024

They gave me options:
Male, Female, or “Prefer not to say.”
Well, I’d prefer to say, but you won’t give me the chance.
How kind of you to box me in.

“Just pick one,” you said.
“You’re a guy, right?”
Oh, how simple.
You look at a profile picture and suddenly you’re an expert.

“Mentally ill?”
Please, that’s the best you’ve got?
How original.
Newsflash: the world’s bigger than your two genders and half a brain cell.

And what’s this?
“Waste of sperm,”
“Handicapped male,”
The internet’s finest scholars at work!
Tell me, do you recycle all that trash you speak, or does it come out fresh every time?

“Why do you care?” you ask.
Let me educate you, since someone has to.
It matters because I exist,
and I refuse to shrink for your comfort.

But look at you,
so pressed about what’s in my pants,
so obsessed with boxes you’ll never fit in.

“Mentally ill?”
No, darling,
I’m mentally awake,
which is more than I can say for you.

I see you,
desperate to drag me down,
but you can’t win against someone
who doesn’t play by your tired rules.

Representation matters.
Not because you think it shouldn’t
but because it rattles your cage.

I get it
You’re confused.
The world’s changing faster than your outdated opinions.
But guess what? We’re here to stay.
Keep your hate,
your ignorance,
your fragile little world.
I’ll be over here,
living my truth,
while you wonder why I never cared what you had to say.

Jayden C
