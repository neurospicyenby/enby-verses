Cover Boy Pretty

Published on 14th November 2024

I Call him Cover Boy Pretty -
the kind of beauty that leaves a ghost in your throat, lingering, sweet and impossible to swallow.
He spills in like honeyed light,
the kind of calm that wraps around you,
even as your pulse skips a beat.

He has that Taurus heat,
Steady, simmering beneath the skin,
a quiet storm with eyes that Say
Come here, let me tell you everything you never dared to ask.
He's kind, funny and his words slip through
with an effortless charm I trip over,
like a song I'm trying to learn by heart.

I'm rubbish at replying,
at holding him with the same warmth he pours into me.
The notifications stack up, and I wonder if he knows he's the one who has me fumbling like this, heart in a chokehold.

I picture his long hair spilling over pillows,
and I'm jealous of the air that gets closer to him than I do.
I'm jealous of the thought of others close to him, brushing past like whispers I can't silence

He's dirty, deliciously so,
a pull that leaves me breathless,
Every word from him feels like a spark,
an invitation, a whispered dare,
drawing me deeper, bearing me wanting more.

He tells me about the shows he's watching,
the worlds he slips into, characters he's made his own.
I listen closely, caught in the way he speaks,
like each story is a doorway,
a piece of him he's letting me hold.

But here I am, replying too late,
holding back too much, holding on too tight
to the words I meant to say,
thinking about him in the quiet hours,
where he's Cover Boy Pretty, and I'm wishing I could hold him in more than just a screen.

Jayden C
