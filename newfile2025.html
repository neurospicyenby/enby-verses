New File: 2025

Published on 31st December 2024


Click: "View History."
2024—ready for archive.
The files are nearly complete:
Laughter saved in cherished folders,
Grief hidden in locked sections,
Lessons marked as “To Be Reviewed.”

It joins the archive of every version before.
A timeline of who I’ve been.
But no archive stays untouched.
2025 will pull forward the hidden unknowns:
Forgotten memories that slip between the cracks,
Feelings long buried that find their way back,
And fragments of strength I didn’t know I saved.

The new year isn’t a clean slate.
It’s another version,
updated, imperfect.
Carrying the best and worst of what came before,
And ready to be written.

Here’s to 2025:
To the files I’ll create,
The ones I’ll keep,
And the ones I’ll let go.

Version 2025—loading.
Jayden C
